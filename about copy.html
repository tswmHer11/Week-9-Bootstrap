Where it all started


Overland Express came to life when a group of friends had dreams of turning their everyday vehicles into adventure mobiles. They were experienced engineers who wanted the ability to take their vehicles on weekend adventures and also use them as their daily drivers. With there experience and enginuity, they started creating products for their own vehicles. They liked it so much, they decided to make their own company knowing there were people who shared their dreams but didn't know how or where to look.

We are a small company that specialize in creating Overlanding vehicles for our customers. You can purchase one or more of our many products or for an additional fee we can custom fit or make something you desire. Our employees consists experienced professional welders, fabricators and engineers that may assist you in finding what's best for your vehicle.
